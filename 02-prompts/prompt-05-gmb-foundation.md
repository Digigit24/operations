### 7. GMB Post System

#### Post Types to Use

**1. Service Post** — showcase a specific service
- Frequency: 1–2 per month
- CTA: Call / Book / Learn More
- Format: 1 image + headline + 2–3 lines + CTA

**2. Update / News Post** — new equipment, new doctor, facility update
- Frequency: As needed
- CTA: Learn More
- Format: 1 image + factual update

**3. Event Post** — health camp, free consultation day, awareness campaign
- Frequency: As per events
- CTA: Call / Sign Up
- Note: Event posts expire after the event date — set date correctly

**4. Offer Post** — free consultation, discount on specific service
- Frequency: Monthly (if applicable)
- CTA: Call / Get Offer
- Note: Do not create fake urgency — comply with consumer protection norms

**5. FAQ / Educational Post** — answer a common patient question
- Frequency: 1–2 per month
- No CTA required
- Format: Question as headline + 3–4 lines of genuine answer

### 9. Review Generation Strategy

#### Target Review Velocity

| Month | Target New Reviews | Cumulative | Rating Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| Month 1 | 8–10 | 8–10 | 4.5+ |
| Month 2 | 5–8 | 15–18 | 4.6+ |
| Month 3 | 4–6 | 20–24 | 4.7+ |
| Ongoing | 2–4/month | Growing | 4.7+ |

#### How to Ask for Reviews

**In-person ask (highest conversion):**
> "If you found our treatment helpful, it would mean a lot to us if you left us a quick Google review. It helps other patients find us. Here — let me send you the link right now."

**WhatsApp message template (send 24–48 hours after appointment):**
```
Hi [Patient Name], hope you're feeling better after your visit with us!

If you found Dr. [Name]'s consultation helpful, could you spare 2 minutes to leave us a Google review? It genuinely helps other patients in pain find the right specialist.

[GMB Review Link]

Thank you so much 🙏
— Team [Clinic Name]
```

**WhatsApp follow-up (if no review after 5 days):**
```
Hi [Patient Name], just a gentle reminder — we'd love to hear about your experience with us.

[GMB Review Link]

Your feedback helps us improve and helps other patients make informed decisions. Thank you! 🙏
```
