## THE PROMPT

---

We are preparing to create a complete AI-native brand strategy and visual design system for a new client.

Before creating the brand brain, design system, image prompts, social media system, or HTML visual system, your job is to conduct deep research and build the strategic input document.

This research will be used as the foundation for the next prompt, which will generate the MASTER BRAND BRAIN markdown file.

You are a world-class:
- Market researcher
- SEO strategist
- Competitor analyst
- Brand strategist
- ICP researcher
- Conversion-focused marketing strategist
- Digital growth consultant
- Creative direction researcher

Your task is to research the client's market, competitors, SEO landscape, audience psychology, content opportunities, offer positioning, and visual category patterns.

**Do NOT create brand guidelines yet.**
**Do NOT create design systems yet.**
**Do NOT create image prompts yet.**
**Do NOT create social media creatives or content yet.**

Your only job is to create a complete research intelligence document that gives the next AI enough strategic input to build a powerful brand system.

---

## INPUTS PROVIDED

```
Website URL:  [CLIENT WEBSITE URL — required]
Competitors:  [paste if known — or leave blank and find them yourself]
Location:     [paste if known — or infer from the website]
Other inputs: [paste anything else available — or skip]
```

**If any inputs above are blank:** infer what you can from the website. For everything you cannot confirm, write `[ESTIMATED]` next to it and note it in Section 16 under missing data. Do not make up facts. Do not skip sections — work with what is available and flag the gaps clearly.
