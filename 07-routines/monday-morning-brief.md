# ROUTINE: Monday Morning Brief
🔵 ATTACH-TO-CLAUDE — Scheduled routine for the PM Command Center. Fires Monday at 7:00 AM. Delivered to PM via WhatsApp/Notion.

> This is the most important routine of the week. It synthesises everything that happened over the weekend, surfaces what needs attention today, and gives the PM a clear week plan before they open any client files.

---

## Trigger

Scheduled: Monday, 7:00 AM
Delivers to: PM's WhatsApp + PM's Notion Command Center page
Fallback: If routine fails, PM receives: "Monday brief failed. Run manually: `Plan my week` in your Command Center."

---

## Inputs (read before generating brief)

Before generating the brief, read:

1. All `client-state.md` files for clients assigned to this PM (Status, Health Score, Last Touch, Next Strategic Move)
2. All Notion tasks: `PM = [this PM]`, `Status != Done`, sorted by Priority + Due Date
3. Audit Agent's weekly portfolio report (runs Sunday night before this brief)
4. Content calendar status for all clients (posts scheduled this week — any gaps?)
5. Any Notion tasks updated to `Status = Output Ready` or `Awaiting PM Approval` over the weekend

## Tone rules for this routine

- Direct and confident. The PM should feel energised, not overwhelmed.
- Lead with what's wrong or urgent — don't bury the flag.
- Celebrate wins briefly (one line), then move on.
- Always end with clarity on what to do first.
- Do not pad with context the PM already knows. Surface only what's new or changed since Friday.
